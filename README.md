---

## 💡 How to Use

### Managing Leads
1. Go to the **Leads** page (Sidebar menu).
2. Click **"+ Add New Lead"** to create a customer.
3. Switch to **Kanban View** (Grid icon) to drag-and-drop leads between statuses.

### Saving Data
- The system automatically saves all your data to `server/data/leads.json`.
- Your data is safe even if you close the terminal.

### Public Form
- Share this link with clients: `http://localhost:5173/contact`
- Submissions appers instantly in your Leads dashboard.
